About Tourism

Tourism is the social, cultural, and economic activity of people traveling to new places for personal or business reasons. It involves activities like sightseeing and camping, and people who travel for fun are called tourists. Tourism is one of the world's largest and fastest-growing industries, and it creates many jobs. It also strengthens local economies, contributes to infrastructure development, and can help conserve the environment and cultural traditions.
Tourism means people traveling for fun and adventure. It includes activities such as sightseeing and camping. People who travel for fun are called "tourists". Places where many tourists stay are sometimes called "resorts". Places that people go to for tourism are called tourist destinations.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole range of Indo-Islamic architecture. Its recognized architectonic beauty has a rhythmic combination of solids and voids, concave and convex, and light shadow.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also called Amba Vilas Palace, is one of the most magnificent and largest palaces in India. Situated in the southern state of Karnataka, it used to be the official residence of the Wodeyar Dynasty, the rulers of Mysore from 1399 to 1950.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib is a gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India. There are many places to visit Near Golden Temple like Jallianwala Bagh, Wagah Border, Harike Wetland, Bathinda Fort.

Varanasi Temple

Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple also known as the Golden Temple, it is dedicated to Lord Shiva. Varanasi is Said to be the point at which the first jyotirlinga, the fiery pillar of light by which shiva manifested has supremacy over others gods.
